Deuce & Advantage — Part 1 of 3

Learning Objective:
Learn the concepts of deuce and advantage through an interactive tennis game simulation.
What is a Deuce?

When both players have 40 points, the game enters a deuce.

What is an Advantage?

After a deuce, a player must win 2 consecutive points to win the game.
Remember the win-by-2 points rule!
