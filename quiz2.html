Quiz: Deuce Scenario

PROGRESS

The score is 40-40 (Deuce). Player B wins one point. What's the new score?

Correct! See explanation below.
Incorrect! Try again.

Explanation:

  • Advantage B — Yes! When the score is deuce (40-40) and a player wins a point, they gain the advantage. If Player B wins the next point, they win the game.
  • 40-30 — No, this would only be possible if the previous score had been 40-30 instead of 40-40 (Deuce).
  • Game B — Not yet! Player B needs to win one more point after advantage to secure the game.
