In which of the following cases would a tiebreak be played in tennis?

Correct! See explanation below.
Incorrect! Try again.

Explanation:

  • 5–5 — No tiebreak yet. Players continue playing.
  • 6–6 — Yes, a tiebreak is played at 6–6 to decide the set.
  • 7–6 — No tiebreak here. The set has already been won 7–6.
  • 5–4 — No tiebreak. Players must reach 6 games with a 2-game margin.
